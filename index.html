Who I am

Hello my name is Miguel Hernandez, I am a Front End Web Developer from New York. Currently residing in Las Vegas. My goal is simple – to ensure beautiful, usable interfaces through handcrafted semantic code that produces a satisfying experience for every visitor to your website. While my vision is noble, I back up that vision with reliable, trustworthy, professional service, high-quality industry competence, and unparalleled creativity and passion that make a distinct difference in the designs I create.

Services

Your website is a sum of layers—structure, data, design, content, and functionality. Creating that user-facing functionality is my job as your front-end developer. Using a combination of markup languages, design, and client-side scripts and frameworks, as the front-end developer I create the environment for everything that users see and touch: content, layout, and interaction. It’s helpful to have a broad view of how the front end of a site functions, but I am here to facilitate that process.

  • Bringing a designer’s concept to life with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Production, modification, and maintenance of websites
  • Creating tools that enhance how users see and interact with your site in any browser
  • Implementing responsive design for mobile sites
  • Contributing some back-end experience, collaborating on APIs, and more
  • Testing the site during development for usability and fixing any bugs
